Posters for Infragistics

May 2013

This project was a nice change from my day-to-day activities at Infragistics, I got the opportunity to design a few posters for the office that reflect the activities of our in-house interest groups. Three poster designs in total, for the Cooking, Book and User Experience clubs. The goal of the posters is not only to popularize the groups themselves, but to have a subtle recruitement note as well.
