Omniamp

Ongoing

Omniamp is an ongoing casual side project that I’m doing with a friend. It started for no apparent reason other than to toy around with the HTML5 Audio API and have fun building something. We were inspired by the retirement of Winamp, our beloved audio player that stopped being supported in 2013. Currently the project is in Alpha and offers very basic functionality—you can add mp3s from your computer, import audio from Soundcloud and listen.
